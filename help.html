Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is JusConsultus AI?

JusConsultus AI is an AI-powered legal research platform designed specifically for Philippine law. It provides access to a comprehensive database of laws, jurisprudence, and legal documents, combined with an intelligent AI assistant to help you find and understand legal information quickly and efficiently.

Is JusConsultus AI a substitute for legal advice?

No. JusConsultus AI is a legal research and information tool, not a substitute for professional legal advice. While our platform helps you find and understand legal information, you should always consult a qualified attorney for legal matters that require professional judgment.

How current is your legal database?

We strive to keep our database as current as possible. Our team regularly updates the database with new laws, issuances, and Supreme Court decisions. However, there may be a short delay between the official publication and availability in our system. Always verify critical information with official sources.

Can I use JusConsultus AI on mobile devices?

Yes! JusConsultus AI is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Access your account and conduct legal research from any device with an internet connection.

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier includes basic access to our legal database with up to 10 searches per day and 5 AI chat queries. You can browse laws and view basic document information. Premium features like unlimited searches, full document access, document generation, and priority support require a paid subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. If you need assistance, contact our support team.

How accurate is the AI assistant?

Our AI assistant is designed to provide helpful and accurate information based on our legal database. However, like all AI systems, it may occasionally make errors or provide incomplete information. Always verify AI responses against primary legal sources, especially for important legal matters.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. Your uploaded documents are stored securely and are only accessible to you. See our Privacy Policy for more details.
